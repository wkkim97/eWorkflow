Bring Your Own Smartphone (BYOS)
Terms and Conditions of Participation

Scope

Bring Your Own Smartphone (BYOS) provides company calendar information, contacts, tasks, mail and access to personal and shared Bayer data in the intranet. This is implemented by an application or a set of applications installed on your device.

This application is a secure container separating business from private content. The secure container is managed by Bayer. In order to access the secure container and content, an alphanumeric passcode is required. The application is named “Capsule” in the following. The service is exclusively available for devices running a certified operating service version provided by Apple or Google. Further details and minimum system requirements are part of the user’s manual (Android, Apple).

The following terms and conditions apply when participating in the services BYOS. Providing the service to an employee depends on his/her commitment to be compliant with the underlying rules. Bayer reserves the right to stop the service, if this is not ensured.

Participant’s Use of the Devices

  • Manage the password to access the Capsule appropriately, select a hard to guess one and don’t share it. Keep it secret.
  • Don’t share or misuse the ActiveSync ID. The ActiveSync ID is used to identify and authorize the device.
  • Lock the Capsule whenever it is not needed or when you pass on your device to others.
  • Be compliant with all applicable Bayer Policies regarding the use and handling of confidential and secret business information including data privacy and data security requirements.
  • Do not store any Bayer information outside of the Capsule.
  • Without limiting the generality of the foregoing, do not store Bayer confidential, secret or business information in external or cloud-based file management services such as Evernote, Dropbox, Google Drive, etc.
  • Store only business information inside the Capsule which is synchronized to controlled environments.
  • Report loss of the device immediately to +49 214 30-99959. The number is operated 24/7 (Local IT helpdesk may support in ensuring that this global service is executed.)
  • Report on termination of use of the device (e.g., if sold or damaged) or service (e.g., if a new device is planned to be used)
  • Do not use privately purchased apps for business use, License conditions may differ between private and business purpose.
  • Do not root/jailbreak the device.

Additional Security Aspects

  • It is recommended to secure the device with a device passcode.
  • It is highly recommended to keep the operation system as well as apps of the non-capsule part up-to-date to ensure that available security corrections are applied.
  • When installing and using apps in the non-capsule part, you should be careful with grantig apps access to your data or personal information like geolocation data, contact information, photo, etc. for your own security.
